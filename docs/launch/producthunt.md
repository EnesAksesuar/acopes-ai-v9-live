## Short Description

## Maker Comment Draft

Hi Product Hunt. I built ACOPES AI after seeing how many Etsy sellers struggle with the same problem: listings may have good products, but weak titles, unclear thumbnails, and low-confidence optimization decisions.

ACOPES AI is a public beta for Etsy sellers who want a controlled AI workflow. It analyzes listings, scores SEO and CTR potential, suggests cleaner Etsy-style titles, checks thumbnail quality, estimates revenue impact, and keeps everything draft-safe until the seller approves it.

This beta is intentionally focused on practical seller workflows:

- title and tag optimization
- thumbnail intelligence
- confidence scoring
- competitor intelligence
- trend and viral potential signals
- queue and credit-based optimization

I would love feedback from Etsy sellers, indie makers, and SaaS builders before the paid version launches.

## Launch Bullets
