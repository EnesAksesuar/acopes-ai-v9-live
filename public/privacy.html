Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 20, 2026

Overview

ACOPES AI is beta software for draft-safe Etsy listing optimization. The product helps sellers review AI-assisted title, description, tag, thumbnail, and scoring suggestions before making any live marketplace changes.

Information We Collect

During public beta, the app may store your email address, shop name, session identifier, usage credits, waitlist submission, listing metadata, optimization history, queue status, and automation logs needed to operate the dashboard.

How We Use Data

We use data to provide draft-safe optimization previews, process Make/Canva automation responses, track credits, maintain session access, debug failed runs, improve scoring quality, and prepare public beta feedback.

Automation And Third Parties

Optimization requests may be sent to connected automation services such as Make.com and Canva export workflows. Do not submit sensitive personal information inside listing descriptions, titles, tags, or automation prompts.

No Guaranteed Sales

ACOPES AI provides optimization suggestions and scoring signals. We do not guarantee Etsy traffic, ranking, clicks, conversions, revenue, or sales outcomes.

Beta Software

This is public beta software. Features, scoring logic, integrations, limits, and data handling may change as the product develops.
